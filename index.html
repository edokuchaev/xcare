Ksenia Dokuchaeva

Montessori Educator & Consultant

What I Do

School Administration

School management, team 0-6 development, admission and onboarding, family community building.

0–3 Program Development

Planning and organizing the Montessori environment and didactic materials, hiring and developing teachers, leading parent–infant and parent–toddler programs.

Family Counseling

Creating Montessori-inspired home environments, navigating communication and developmental challenges.

Expertise

  • Co-founder & Director Founded and managed an authentic Montessori school in Moscow, Russia, serving 150 children ages 0–12 since 2014.
  • School Operations Led family admissions, parent relations, team development, and community building.
  • International Consultant Helped launch new Montessori schools in Bali, Serbia, Portugal, Uzbekistan, and the United Arab Emirates.
  • Family Counseling Led workshops and individual consultations on nonviolent communication, child adaptation to school, and creating a Montessori home environment.
  • Formal Education Master of Education, Loyola University Maryland; AMI 0–3 Training, The Montessori Institute, Denver.
