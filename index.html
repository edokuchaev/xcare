What I Do

School Leadership

I am open to opportunities in Montessori school leadership and management, particularly in admissions, parent relations, team development, and community building.

Communities & Families

I work with parents, schools, and Montessori communities to create thoughtful environments where children can grow with confidence, freedom, and responsibility.

Parent Consultations

My work includes parent consultations on the home environment, communication challenges, child development, developmental crises, Montessori at home, and adaptation to childcare or kindergarten.

Seminars & Training

I also offer seminars and practical support for new schools and for schools interested in Montessori principles, with topics including the prepared environment for ages 0–6 and 6–12, communication with young children, observation, Montessori fundamentals, and I enjoy leading parent-infant and parent-toddler programs that strengthen the connection between the home and the school.
