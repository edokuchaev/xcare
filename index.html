Ksenia Dokuchaeva

Montessori Educator & Consultant

What I Do

School Leadership

I am open to joining a Montessori school in a leadership or management role, with a particular interest in programs for children up to age 6, parent engagement, team development, and community building.

0-3 Program Development

I am deeply inspired by the first years of life and the creation of environments for children aged 0–3. I support schools in developing toddler communities, and I enjoy leading parent-infant and parent-toddler programs that strengthen the connection between the home and the school.

Parent Consultations

I support families in creating thoughtful Montessori-inspired home environments and navigating communication and developmental challenges with young children through individual consultations and parent workshops.

School Consultations

For schools at different stages – from those just starting to those looking to align more deeply with Montessori principles – I provide seminars and hands-on support in areas such as the prepared environment (0–6), observation, and nonviolent communication. I also share my experience of creating and developing a Montessori school from the ground up.
